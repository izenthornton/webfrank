What we do

FRANK Autonomous Systems builds the autonomy layer for planetary rovers. Mission 1 puts the first autonomous swarm on the Moon: hazard mapping and site-characterisation surveys first, subsurface mineral prospecting second. Ground-truth data the lunar economy needs.
